üè∑ About Me

I am a Professor at the School of Artificial Intelligence and Big Data, Hefei University, and have been awarded the title of "Outstanding Young Graduate Supervisor in Anhui Province." My research endeavors focus on advancing the frontiers of structural pattern recognition, machine learning, graph spectral analysis, image and graph matching, with a particular emphasis on kernel methods and the complexity analysis of graphs and networks.
